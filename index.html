AI Governance Framework

Activity Group Activity Expected Evidence
Section 1 1.1 Objectives of deploying AI 1.Documentation of the defined objectives and goals for deploying AI within the organization or project. 2.Strategic plans or project charters outlining the intended outcomes and benefits of AI deployment. 3.Alignment of AI objectives with the broader organizational goals and mission statements.
Section 2 2.1 Internal governance structures and measures 1.Organizational charts or descriptions of internal governance structures related to AI deployment. 2.Policies, procedures, or guidelines governing the use of AI within the organization. 3.Records of training programs or educational materials provided to staff regarding AI governance.
Section 3 3.1 Determining the level of human involvementin AI-augmented decision-making 1.Documentation outlining the decision-making processes where AI is involved and the level of human oversight or intervention required. 2.Risk assessments or impact analyses assessing the potential consequences of AI-augmented decision-making. 3.Reports or studies evaluating the effectiveness of human-in-the-loop or human-on-the-loop approaches in AI systems.
Section 4 4.1 Operations management 1.Operational plans or workflows detailing how AI systems are integrated into daily operations. 2.Records of system monitoring, maintenance, and performance evaluation procedures. 3.Incident reports or logs documenting any issues, failures, or unexpected outcomes related to AI operations.
Section 5 5.1 Stakeholder Interaction and Communication 1.Stakeholder engagement plans outlining how stakeholders are involved in AI governance processes. 2.Records of stakeholder consultations, meetings, or feedback sessions regarding AI deployment. 3.Communication materials such as reports, newsletters, or presentations explaining AI initiatives and their impacts to stakeholders.
